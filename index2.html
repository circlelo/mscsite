Welcome to Mask Shiba Club

APG is a collection of 8888 art pieces that will be minted & stored upon the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 tokens. After the initial minting phase, they will continue to be on sale and tradable via OpenSea. Membership & Roadmap perks for Mask Shiba Club are exclusive to APG NFT holders only.

Faq

What’s the Mask Shiba Club?

APG is a collection of 8888 programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain.

When is the release date?

Penguins are eager to get into the blockchain, so it will happen very soon. Join our Discord community to keep up to date.

Why get Angry Penguins?

High-level art, strong community, regular contests, giveaways, and exciting events for our fellow penguins! In addition, 10% of royalties after sold out goes to the fund to help victims of scam projects. Are you still thinking?

Where are the images stored?

All images and metadata are stored in IPFS.

Alright, how much?

1 NFT per wallet for 0 ETH. Yes, that’s exactly it! Everyone can mint 1 NFT for free. You only pay for gas. And there are 2 more options for those who want to mint more penguins: 3 NFTs for 0.04 ETH + gas and 10 NFTs for 0.1 ETH + gas. They will then be available on OpenSea.io

How do I mint an Angry Penguin?

You’ll only be able to mint from this site using Metamask extension for your browser.

Are there secondary sale royalties?

7.5% of the secondary sales will be deducted in royalties. After all APG have been sold, we will use them to:

  • — up to 30% for marketing part and sustainable development of the project, to make sure that the project continues to grow,
  • — 10% goes to the holders suffered from the scam projects,
  • — at least 5% will be returned to the community with prize payments of upcoming contests and raffles,
  • — at least 25% will serve as a budget for a subsequent project with drops for eligible APG holders
  • — and 30% will go to the team

When Is The Reveal?

APG will be revealed in a few hours after mint start.

What can I do with my Angry Penguin?

You are free to do anything with them under a non-exclusive license.

what does eligible APG holder imply?

Having at least 1 APG NFT makes you a full-fledged member of our grotto and gives you access to events and raffles for all APG owners. But we recommend minting at least 3 pengos because in this case you are guaranteed to get a free drop from the subsequent collection.

Are Mask Shiba Club a good investment?

A long-term approach to project development in conjunction with significantly lower mint prices compared to similar projects allows us to believe that this is most likely to be a good investment, but ultimately, it is your decision that you have to make.
